Change is upon us!

In this ever evolving world of change, technological advancement and continuous improvement, it is becoming increasingly critical for mining and resources companies to turn their attention towards better understanding their data, systems and processes.

Why Mine Mage?

Built upon the “if you can’t measure it you can’t manage it” philosophy, Mine Mage is a reporting tool that gives organisations better access to their data so they can start to understand emerging trends, quickly identify inefficiencies and closely monitor the performance of their fleet down to granular detail.

Data at your Fingertips

To enable quick and effective decision making, decision makers need up to date real-time data at their fingertips. Mine Mage has been designed to integrate with any device over any platform, so your reports are always just a single click away. With the possibility of cloud connectivity, you can potentially view your data anywhere, at any time, from anywhere in the world.

Stunning Visuals

Designed around Six Sigma and Lean Principles, Mine Mage is designed to draw quick attention to key leading indicators so that performance improvement interventions can be implemented promptly. Custom built reports and downloadable data also enables companies to perform additional analysis if needed.

Custom Built

We recognise that all organisations are different, which is why we have built a high degree of flexibility into the software to ensure it can meet the needs of any operation. Mine Mage’s reports and dashboards are custom built for your site so the possibilities are endless!
